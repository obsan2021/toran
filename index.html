Our Story

Born in Adama, Loved by All

Toran Burger was founded with a single mission: to bring world-class fast food flavors to the heart of Adama, Ethiopia. Tucked inside the iconic Batu Tower on Franko Street, we've been serving the city's most talked-about burgers, pizzas, and burritos since day one.

Every item on our menu is made fresh to order — from our signature King Size Special Burger stacked with all the fixings, to our crispy KFC-style fried chicken and wood-fired-style pizzas. We believe great food shouldn't require a plane ticket.

Whether you're grabbing a quick lunch between lectures at ASTU, celebrating a milestone, or just craving something extraordinary — Toran is your spot.
